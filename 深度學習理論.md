### Concept

#### Convolution

- 公式推導

    $$
    \begin{aligned}
    & L: Origin\ Length\\
    & p: padding\\
    & f: filter\ Length\\
    & s: stride\\
    & N: New\ Length\\
    &\\
    &L+2p = f \times N -(f-s)(N-1)\\
    &L=f+s(N-1)-2p\\
    &\implies N = \frac{L-f+2p}{s}+1\\
    &\end{aligned}
    $$
